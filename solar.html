Solar Energy

  • Solar power is energy from the sun that is converted into thermal or electrical energy
  • Solar energy is the cleanest and most abundant renewable energy source available
  • Modern technology can harness this energy for a variety of uses, including generating electricity, providing light or a comfortable interior environment, and heating water for domestic, commercial, or industrial use


Tesla & Solar City

Tesla and Solar City are innovative companies, that are trying to create solution for households, industry and transportation to be able to use solar (electric) energy easily, convenient and affordable.

Households

  • In 2015, the average annual electricity consumption for a U.S. residential utility customer was 10,812 kilowatthours (kWh), an average of 901 kWh per month that is 30 kWh per day
  • A typical solar panel produces about 200 watts of electricity, with 25 pannels like this installed on a house the output is 5 kWh
  • If every house had solar pannels it would be theoretically self efficient

Night time = No Energy



  • Sun does not shine during a night
  • The highest demand of energy in households is usually in the morning and in the evening
  • It seems like we cannot really use the solar energy from our pannels
  • Tesla and Solar City have solution to this problem

Powerwall



  • Tesla's solution comprise of installing high capacity batteries into your house
  • The Powerwall is being charged during a day and can provide energy when solar pannels are not producing electricity
  • In case of outage of global power grid powerwall provides backup solution
  • The capacity of the powerwall is 13.5 kWh which is usually enough for supplying power for lights, sockets and fridge
  • It is also possible to stack more Powerwalls if more capacity is needed
  • Tesla gives 10 years warranty on their Powerwall product

Affordability



  • In the past the cost of solar pannels was high and most of the households could not afford such a solution
  • Nowadays the price per wat generated by solar pannels is decreasing annualy
  • Solar City also offer leases for the people without sufficient financial resources

Attractivity



  • Typical solar pannels can be considered ugly accessory to the roof
  • Last product of Solar City called Solar Roof combines solar pannel into the classic roof tile
  • They provide different styles to fit everybody's needs
  • Solar City claims that the durability of solar roof tiles is bigger than the standard ones
  • It is made of extremely strong tempered glass which can withstand the impact of hail up to 200 mph.
  • They can take on nearly three times the force of standard roofing tiles

Industrial facilities

Powerpack



Powerpack supports a host of applications that offer commercial consumers and energy providers greater control, efficiency and reliability across the electric grid.

  • Powerwall like solution for bigger scale
  • High-performance batteries for the grid
  • State of the art battery system designed for efficiency and long life.
  • Flexible, infinitely scalable modular layout.

Scalability



The Powerpack system scales to the space, power and energy requirements of any site, from small commercial businesses to regional utilities. It can be configured in various arrangements, offering far more modularity than competing models.

Every Powerpack contains 16 individual battery pods, each with an isolated DC-DC converter. Pod architecture and onboard power electronics optimize performance across the array and enable easy swapping at any time.

Solar Island



The island of Ta’u in American Samoa is now virtually energy independent thanks to a new solar and battery installation by Tesla and SolarCity

The company deployed a 1.4-megawatt solar array and a 6-megawatt hour energy storage system with 60 Tesla Powerpacks. The system is what is called a microgrid and it’s now the island’s main source of energy.

This is a great example of strenght of solar energy and when used in combination with battery system it is usable in massive scale.

Tesla Cars

Electric cars are not something new. They were here many years ago. The problem was they were very unattractive with short mileage range.

Tesla's first product was Tesla Roadster which was an electric sport car. Tesla Roadster was attractive car with acceptable mileage range of 320 km.

Roadster was higher class car that was electric and very attractive. Tesla had earned reasonable money with Roadster sales which could pay back the research.

Model S & Model X



The next product of Teslaw was more afforddable model S which was stil higher class vehicle but provided enviromental replacement for cars like Audi A4, VW Passat, BMW series 5.

Short after Model S tesla unveiled Model X. Both models were very popular in quite affordable price. This way tesla changed the population's oppinion about electric cars

Model 3



The lates product of Tesla is Model 3 which is mid class vehicle with very affordable price. Stil provides the performance and safety on the same level as the previous models.

With this approach tesla moved their businness from high class sport cars to mid class cars affordable to families. The population now has opprotunity to buy enviroment friendly electric car for reasonable price.

Supercharger



Superchargers are free connectors that charge Tesla cars in minutes instead of hours. Stations are strategically placed to minimize stops during long distance travel.

Tesla claims that use of superchargers is and wil be free for lifetime for every Tesla's car. This is very attractive offer since people can drive practicaly for free if they are charging their cars only at superchargers.

Gigafactory

Almost every product of Tesla and Solar City requires use of batteries. The batteries production process has also enviromental impact.

With a planned production rate of 500,000 cars per year in the latter half of this decade, Tesla alone will require today’s entire worldwide production of lithium ion batteries. The Tesla Gigafactory was born of necessity and will supply enough batteries to support our projected vehicle demand.

Gigafactory will reach full capacity and produce more lithium ion batteries annually than were produced worldwide in 2013.

The Gigafactory will also be powered by renewable energy sources, with the goal of achieving net zero energy.

Tesla and its partners intend to complete the facility before 2020.

Solarcity also claims that for each ton of carbon emitted in 2015 carbon footprint, the clean energy systems that they have deployed avoided more than 3 tons. The whole enviromental impact is then impressive.

Conclusion

Tesla’s approach changed the way people think about solar energy. Electric cars became super attractive. Solar roofs more affordable than ever before. Together with powerwall sold as a bundle it is a simple solution for households. The more households will use power from renewebla energy like this the lower enviromental impact the electricity production will have.

There are real examples of effectiveness and usability of Tesla's product like mentioned Ta’u island electricity solution.

We consider Tesla & Solar city as a pearl of ICT4D related to climate change not only because of the technologies the company uses, but because of the approach and the company politics that shapes the world in the global scale into using renewable electricity.
