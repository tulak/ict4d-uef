What is climate change ?

Climate is usually defined as the "average weather" in a place. It includes patterns of temperature, precipitation (rain or snow), humidity, wind and seasons.

Rising levels of carbon dioxide and other heat-trapping gases in the atmosphere have warmed the Earth and are causing wide-ranging impacts, including rising sea levels; melting snow and ice; more extreme heat events, fires and drought; and more extreme storms, rainfall and floods.

Why is it happening ?

Human cause

  • Electricity and heat generation
  • Transportation sector
  • Industrial sector
  • Residental
  • Others

Natural cause

  • Ocean-atmosphere exchange
  • Plant and animal respiration
  • Soil respiration and decomposition
  • Volcanic eruptions

What can we do about it ?

Most of the CO2 pollution comes from fossil fuels consuption.

Naive approach: Let's stop using fossil fuels

Well it's not that easy

Doable solutions

Minimize the polution from using coal

Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) is a technology that can capture up to 90% of the carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions produced from the use of fossil fuels in electricity generation and industrial processes, preventing the carbon dioxide from entering the atmosphere.

Use as much renewable energy as we can

By using solar energy for various purposes we can minimize the neccesity of using fossil fuels.

Sun is powerfull source of energy and we should use it as much as possible. However there are several challenges that need to be considered when using solar energy like energy storing.

Conclusion

Carbon dioxide emission is the main cause of global warming. We need to reduce it however we know is not possible to get rid of it totally. The presented projects solves some parts of the problem of CO2 emission however niether of them is a silver bullet solution.

By investing time, money and effort to the projects like this we help our planet to survive human race impact on the environment.
